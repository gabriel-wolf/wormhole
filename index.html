Our Universe

A common phrase we will be using is "brane"
Our entire world is contained in a 2D brane which we call "our universe" or "our brane"
This concept is key to understanding the rest of this site.

Bulk

Popular media usually calls the bulk "hyperspace." Star Wars is a great example.

Han and Chewie jump to "lightspeed" and enter "hyperspace."

Bulk Beings

Bulk Beings are "beings" from other branes (other "dimensions").
Check out this scene from Interstellar where the crew interacts with what they call "bulk beings"

It would be reasonable to assume other branes have their own matter, forces, and fields.
HOWEVER we cannot interact with any of these except gravity and the warping of spacetime due to gravity.

Wormholes

Commonly called an Einstein-Rosen Bridge.

Black Holes have one singularity. Wormholes have two singularities.

Singularities

A singularity is a place where spacetime is infinitely warped.

When a wormhole pinches off (collapses), everything inside of it is destroyed.

This prevents realistic use of wormholes for space travel!

Traversable Wormholes

Wormholes that allow travel are called traversable wormholes.
The issue is traversable wormholes can't stay open long enough!

How do we keep traversable wormholes open?

We will explain how we can keep wormholes open longer.
But first, we need to learn about why wormholes collapse in the first place.

Light Rays

Gravitating Bodies like the sun and black holes bend light rays inward.

Negative Masses like wormholes bend light rays outwards.

Negative masses contain what we call negative energy.

Negative Energy
