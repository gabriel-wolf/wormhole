WORMHOLE

Our Universe

A common phrase we will be using is "brane"
Our entire world is contained in a 2D brane which we call "our universe" or "our brane"

This concept is key to understanding the rest of this site.

Bulk

Popular media usually calls the bulk "hyperspace." Star Wars is a great example.

Han and Chewie jump to "lightspeed" and enter "hyperspace."

Bulk Beings

Bulk Beings are "beings" from other branes (other "dimensions").
Check out this scene from Interstellar where the crew interacts with what they call "bulk beings"

It would be reasonable to assume other branes have their own matter, forces, and fields.
HOWEVER, we cannot interact with any of these except gravity and the warping of spacetime due to gravity.

Pass Through (Advanced Topic)

This topic is extremely advanced and beyond the scope of this site.
However, I have done more research and thus have a greater knowledge on this topic than is shown here.
Feel free to ask me more about this or anything else and I can talk to you more about it in depth.

When a bulk being passes through our brane,

We cannot see the contents of the being (because the cross section is transparent)
We can feel it and see it's gravity and warping of spacetime (due to gravity).

This clip shows Cooper using gravity to communicate with the people on Earth from inside the tesseract.

Speaking of warping...

Wormholes

Commonly called an Einstein-Rosen Bridge.

Black Holes have one singularity. Wormholes have two singularities.

Singularities

A singularity is a place where spacetime is infinitely warped.

When a wormhole pinches off (collapses), everything inside of it is destroyed.

This prevents realistic use of wormholes for space travel!

Traversable Wormholes

Wormholes that allow travel are called traversable wormholes.
The issue is traversable wormholes can't stay open long enough!

How do we keep traversable wormholes open?

We will explain how we can keep wormholes open longer.
But first, we need to learn about why wormholes collapse in the first place.

Light Rays

Gravitating Bodies like the sun and black holes bend light rays inward.

Negative Masses like wormholes bend light rays outwards.

Negative masses contain what we call negative energy.

Negative Energy

Exotic Matter (made out of negative energy) is used to keep the mouth of a wormhole open.

we need a whole lot of exotic matter

We would need the energy the sun produces over 100 million years to make a wormhole the size of a grapefruit.
(From Marcus Woo)

Photo Credits: Before for over 100 million years After size of a single grapefruit

Naturally Occurring Wormhole

John Wheeler's theory of quantum foam gives us a possible solution to our problem by the creation of naturally occuring wormholes.

Quantum Foam

Quantum foam or is the quantum fluctuation of spacetime on very small scales due to quantum mechanics.
Many believe that there is a chance that one of these wormholes could grow to human size by random chance.
But is that true?

We don't know. We simply don't know.
